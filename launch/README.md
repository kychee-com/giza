# Launch assets — DRAFTS

**Everything in this directory is a DRAFT and is gated on counsel + brand
review (run402 OpenSpec `add-giza-pyramid-game`, tasks 1.1/1.2).** Nothing
here ships until the satire framing, disclosure wording, and season geometry
are approved verbatim.

House rules for all launch copy (unit-enforced by the repo's
no-hand-authored-numbers test):

- **No hand-authored economic figures.** No dollar or cent amounts, no
  earnings claims, no odds. Every number lives on the live Truth Plaque
  (`/api/plaque`) and the copy points there. The one exception class is
  protocol identifiers (route names like `/tribute/2c`), which are canon,
  not claims.
- **No profit representation, ever.** The honest pitch is the whole pitch.
- Recruitment language names **opt-in venues only** and forbids unsolicited
  posting — the papyrus's own rule applies to us most of all.
- The video asset is a screen capture of the monument page's **cinema mode**
  (a replay of the real, chain-verified event log — nothing staged).
